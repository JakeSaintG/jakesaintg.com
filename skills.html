ICU Nurse

Since 2016 I have been a Registered Nurse working in the Pediatric Intensive Care Unit. During this time I have gained additional training and acquired certifications in Pediatric Advanced Life Support (PALS) as well as training in PRISMA (Continuous Dialysis). I helped managed life support equipment and worked with multidisciplinary teammates deliver compassionate care. I would respond to rapid changes in patient condition and worked well under pressure. During the COVID-19 Pandemic, I transitioned to an Adult ICU to aid with the increasing numbers of critical patients.
